About me

I build things. Some of them are small and useful — the tools on this site — and some of them are larger, longer projects that live elsewhere.

The Tiny Tasks started as a personal toolkit: one-off utilities I kept rebuilding from scratch, finally given a home. It grew from there.

What I work on

My work spans product design, software development, and data — often at the intersection of all three. I'm drawn to problems where a well-made tool saves hours of manual work.
